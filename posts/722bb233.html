It's OK to quit your Ph.D

Your words “one of the difficulties for me is that I didn't find a question that I need to answer”

clearly indicate that you are not the point to apply Ph.D course.

I wrote that you need to grab Ph.D, but it is not something you find easy topic.

You need to have something you want to think and act for your life.

Of course, not all the Ph.D student is like this, but they usually have more time.

Again, I suggest you complete your master degree to find what is research for yourself.

Then you may be able to find your own direction.

I do not think you need to be hurry to finish your Ph.D with me.

慢慢写吧。

上面是我老板写给我的关于博士申请的最终决定的邮件。

最终我还是决定不读了

先来两张图

图上是现在非升即走的流程，最终的结论就是，大部分博后，都是要走的。

我不可能做出超出我认知之外的决定，也许之后高校生态会改善，但是我要根据我手上的信息作出有限信息下的决定了。

不带任何情感偏向，纯从利益考虑。

我决定暂时先退出了。

毕竟这行要跟全世界最聪明的脑子竞争。

而我 也没那么好的脑子了。

我真的好想提前安定下来呀
